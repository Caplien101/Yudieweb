Rumus Perhitungan Achievement:
• Total STD Terjadi = Actual STD/Hari × HK Berjalan
• Total Member Terjadi = Actual Member% × Total STD Terjadi
• Target Member Seharusnya = Target Member% × Total STD Terjadi

• Achievement Member = (Total Member Terjadi / Target Member Seharusnya) × 100%
• Total Achievement = (STD Ach × 50%) + (Member Ach × 50%)
